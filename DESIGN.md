**Where creativity is born.**  
A digital atelier, not a bookstore.

## North star

Treat the site as a **museum-of-the-future × design studio**: an interactive installation that still reads like a magazine. Content stays serious; the frame is playful, asymmetric, and alive.

## Palette

| Role        | Token    | Feel                          |
|------------|----------|-------------------------------|
| Surface    | Cream    | Warm paper, studio light      |
| Energy     | Pink     | Pulse, courage, spark         |
| Depth      | Violet   | Thought, night studio         |
| Clarity    | Cyan     | Flow, signal, focus           |
| Warmth     | Amber    | Gold of craft, finishing light|

Gradients move **pink → violet → cyan → amber**. Prefer CSS variables; avoid flat “shop grey.”

## Layout principles

1. **Gallery rooms, not aisles** — Each shelf is a frosted “studio bay” with a colored wall strip, soft mesh glow, and slight left/right offset on desktop.
2. **Books as objects** — Spines keep hover-tilt + tooltips, but rest at varied angles and tiny height offsets so the runway feels installed, not stocked.
3. **Glass navigation** — Floating pill bar, blur + prism border; primary actions use the pink–violet gradient.
4. **Studio desk modal** — Portfolio sheet: prism top edge, ruled-paper body, dark “soundboard” TTS panel.
5. **Cinematic hero** — Mesh gradients, glass content plate, prism seal, oversized Create wordmark as a light sculpture.

## Motion & a11y

Micro-lifts, spring hover, wave EQ on play. All honor `prefers-reduced-motion`. Focus rings use violet; body text stays high-contrast ink on cream.

## What we refuse

Standard bookshop clones, endless beige, rigid centered columns without rhythm, decorative noise that fights reading.

## One-line brief

**An atelier floor where literature leans, glows, and invites you to open a desk.**
